Location example

The cookie law is not identical everywhere. Each member country must implement their own law. For example, some countries require that a consciopus action is taken (therefore timeouts and "dismiss on scroll" like options cannot be used). Some countries say that an informational banner is sufficient, and others say that websites MUST provide a way to allow the user to refuse consent. Other countries that are in europe but not part of the euopean union are exempt from this law

What we suggest is to assuming the highest level of compliance I.E. Explicit consent caused by a conscious action made directly by the user, and then alter features depending on the legislation of the user's country. That way you are covered for all countries, and in cases where the rules are less strict, we can migrate to less intrusive options.
